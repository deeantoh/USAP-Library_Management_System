ZIMHEPRS User Guide
STEP 1: Option 1| How to enter your qualifications and career prospects for recommendations

1. Start by clicking 'START' on the menu.
2. Enter your A Level Subjects results.
- To add new subject click 'Add subject' button.
- To remove a subject, click the 'Delete' button.
3. Click "Submit Results' to submit your results for recommendation.
4. Choose "O" Level status and your prefered career if any and then click 'Submit' button.
5. Wait for the processing to completed.
6. The recommended programmes will be displayed.

STEP 2 | To view recommended programmes and career prospects

1. Complete STEP 1 first.
2. Click 'View Career Prospects' button to view the career propects on the recommended programmes.

STEP 3 | To apply for a Recommended Programme

1. Complete STEP 1 first.
2. Click 'Apply' Button on recommended programme. You will be redirected to the university online application portal.

STEP 4: Option 2 | How to use a preferred career for recommendations

1. Click the 'Search Career' button on the menu.
2. Choose the career you want or prefer.
3. Click the 'Search' button. The programmes, Duration and Universities that offer that programmes linked to that career will be displayed.
4. Click 'View requirements' button to see requirements for the recommended programmes.
5. To apply for a recommended programme, Click the 'Apply' button.

STEP 6 | To Submit your Feedback and Logout

1. Scroll down to the Feedback option.
2. Choose your option from the list.
3. Click 'Submit' Button to submit your feedback.
4. Click the 'LOGOUT' to log out of the system.
